Mobile Repair Solution

In today’s fast-paced world, our mobile phones are essential tools for communication, work, and entertainment. But what happens when your mobile gets damaged or malfunctions? My project is a Mobile Repair Website designed to simplify the process of repairing damaged mobile phones.

How It Works:

  • The customer visits the website and fills out a simple form to book a repair slot by providing details about their damaged mobile phone

  • After receiving the repair request, the admin (me) carefully checks the damage description

  • Then, I review the request and send a detailed repair estimate back to the customer, specifying the expected repair cost.

  • The customer can then accept or reject the repair bill through the website

  • Once accepted, the repair process begins, and the customer is updated along the way
